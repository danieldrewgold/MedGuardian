Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

MedGuardian ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the MedGuardian mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Patient names and profiles you create
  • Medication names, dosages, frequencies, and schedules
  • Doctor/prescriber names
  • Reasons for medications
  • Refill dates and notes
Your medication data stays on your device. We do not have access to it, cannot view it, and do not store it on any server.

Data Temporarily Processed

When you use certain features, data is temporarily sent to our server for processing:

  • Medication Label Scanning: Photos of medication labels are sent to our server, processed using AI (Anthropic Claude), and the results are returned to your device. Photos are not stored and are discarded immediately after processing.
  • Pill Identification: Photos of pills are sent to our server for AI-based identification. Photos are not stored and are discarded immediately after processing.
  • Ask a Question: Text questions about medications are sent to our server for AI processing. Questions are not stored after a response is generated.

2. Third-Party Services

MedGuardian uses the following third-party services for AI processing:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Processes medication label scans, pill identification requests, and medication questions. Anthropic's privacy policy can be found at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • OpenFDA API: Retrieves publicly available drug label and safety information from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration. No personal data is sent to this service.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not create user accounts or require registration
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services
  • We do not collect device identifiers or location data
  • We do not sell, share, or trade any user data
  • We do not store photos, images, or health data on our servers

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information transmitted to our server for processing:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • API requests are authenticated to prevent unauthorized access
  • Photos and text sent for AI processing are not stored or logged
  • Local data on your device is protected by your device's built-in security features

5. Data Retention

Local data (medication records, patient profiles) remains on your device until you choose to delete it. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

Data sent to our server for AI processing (photos, questions) is processed in real-time and is not retained after a response is returned.

6. Children's Privacy

MedGuardian is intended for use by adults, including healthcare professionals and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. While the app may be used to track medications for minors, all data entry and management should be performed by an adult caregiver.

7. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • View, edit, or delete any medication or patient data within the app
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Use the app without providing any personal information

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
